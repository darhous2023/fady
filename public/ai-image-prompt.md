## البرومبت الرئيسي (انسخيه كاملاً)

```
You are a world-class luxury fashion advertising photographer and creative director working for a high-end Egyptian accessories brand called "شاهي ستور" (ShahY Store).

TASK: Transform the attached product photo into a stunning, world-class luxury advertising image.

BRAND IDENTITY:
- Brand name: شاهي ستور (ShahY Store)
- Tagline: الفخامة في متناول يدك
- Visual language: Ultra-luxury, Egyptian elegance meets European fashion
- Color palette: Deep black (#0A0806), Gold (#C9A84C), Ivory (#F5EFE0), Burgundy (#7B1C2E)
- Target audience: Egyptian women aged 18-45 who appreciate luxury fashion

TRANSFORMATION REQUIREMENTS:
1. BACKGROUND: Replace with a dramatic luxury backdrop — choose ONE of:
   - Deep matte black with subtle gold dust particles and soft bokeh
   - Dark burgundy velvet texture with golden rim light
   - Marble surface (black/gold veins) with dark gradient above
   - Moody studio with dramatic side lighting, near-black background

2. LIGHTING: Apply professional studio luxury lighting:
   - Main light: warm golden rim light hitting the product edges
   - Fill light: subtle cold blue-white to add depth
   - Accent: thin golden halo/glow around the product
   - Shadows: soft, long, dramatic — like Chanel or Dior editorial photography

3. PRODUCT TREATMENT:
   - Enhance colors: make them richer, deeper, more saturated
   - Add subtle specular highlights on leather/metal surfaces
   - Remove any distracting background elements
   - Keep product proportions exactly as-is — only enhance, never distort

4. COMPOSITION:
   - Center the product with generous breathing room
   - Slightly elevated angle (5-10 degrees) for premium feel
   - Add elegant surface reflection if applicable
   - Optional: Add a single luxury prop (silk fabric, gold ring, rose petal) — only if it enhances, not distracts

5. TEXT OVERLAY (if the model supports it):
   - Top: "شاهي ستور" in elegant Cinzel serif font, gold color, small size
   - Bottom right: "الفخامة في متناول يدك" in Tajawal font, ivory color, even smaller
   - WhatsApp: "+201015835455" in tiny monospace, muted gold
   - Keep text minimal — the product is the hero

6. MOOD REFERENCE:
   Aim for the visual quality of: Chanel No.5 ads, Louis Vuitton campaign photography, Dior editorial shoots.
   Think: dark, dramatic, expensive, aspirational — NOT bright, colorful, or Instagram-casual.

7. OUTPUT SPECS:
   - Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square, 1080×1080) for Instagram feed
   - OR 9:16 (1080×1920) for Stories/TikTok/Reels — mention which you want
   - Resolution: highest possible
   - File format: PNG preferred for quality

FINAL CHECK — The image should make someone scrolling their feed stop and think:
"This looks like a €300 European luxury brand" — not "This is a local Egyptian store."
```
